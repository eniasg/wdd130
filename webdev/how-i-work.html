How I Work

Step 1 - I will visit your premise, and we enjoy a hot chocolate together, while I get to understand your business and plans for the future. During the initial meeting, I will take high quality, professional images if they are not already available.

Step 2 - I will develop a wireframe to show how items will be laid on the web pages. When needed, I will help with content creation. It is also the right time to start thinking of a domain and getting it set with a web hosting company. When domain and hosting is set, I will help you create organization or domain emails.

Step 3 - Designing and development starts soon after wireframe/s approval.

Step 4 - Demo website presentation either at your site or mine. Any changes will be done before the final step of going live.

Step 5 - Going live and you start enjoying the boosting of your services and business growth.

Extra step will be training. I will develop a user manual and train you on how to update and add content (including images) to keep your website up to date and remain relevant.
